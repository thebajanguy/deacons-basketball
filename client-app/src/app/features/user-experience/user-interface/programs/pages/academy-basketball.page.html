Deacons Basketball Academy Programs

The Deacons Basketball Academy Programs builds fundamentals: ball-handling, footwork, shooting form, on-ball defense, and game IQ with small-sided games and competitive scrimmages. Academies are conducted daily and deliver structured, age-appropriate youth basketball training led by expert coaches. Each academy focuses on targeted skill progression—take them sequentially for maximum improvement across a player's development. Open to boys and girls ages 11U–19U. Limited spots—call for availability.

FAQs

High-intensity skill training, game IQ, leadership—and competitive reps that translate to real games.


Is the camp co-ed?

Yes. Both male and female young-basketballers are welcome. Sessions group by age and skill level for competitive balance.

Do you offer scholarship opportunities?

Yes. Limited merit- and need-based scholarships may be available. You can indicate interest during registration.

Can beginners attend?

Absolutely. Coaches scale intensity and drills to each athlete’s level—everyone trains to improve.
