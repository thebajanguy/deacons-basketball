Brains & Ballers Soccer Camps

The Brains & Ballers Soccer Camps Series builds fundamentals: dribbling, footwork, finishing, 1v1 defending, and game IQ with small-sided games and competitive scrimmages. Camps run during school breaks. Our Fall, Winter, Spring, and Summer editions deliver structured, age-appropriate youth soccer training led by expert coaches. Each camp targets specific skill progression—take them sequentially for maximum improvement across the season. Open to boys and girls ages 11–18. Limited spots—reserve early.

What you get

High-intensity soccer skill training, game IQ development, leadership sessions, and showcase matches—built to move you up a level.

FAQs

High-intensity training, smarter decision-making, leadership—and competitive reps that translate to real matches.


Is the camp co-ed?

Yes. Both male and female student-athletes are welcome. Sessions group by age and skill level for competitive balance.

Do you offer scholarship opportunities?

Yes. Limited merit- and need-based scholarships may be available. You can indicate interest during registration.

Can beginners attend?

Absolutely. Coaches scale intensity and drills to each athlete’s level—everyone trains to improve.
