Brains & Ballers Basketball Camps

The Brains & Ballers Basketball Camps Series builds fundamentals: ball-handling, footwork, shooting form, on-ball defense, and game IQ with small-sided games and competitive scrimmages. Camps are held during school breaks. Our Fall, Winter, Spring, and Summer camps deliver structured, age-appropriate youth sports training led by expert coaches. Each camp focuses on targeted skill progression—take them sequentially for maximum improvement across the season. Open to boys and girls ages 11–18. Limited spots—reserve early.

What you get

High-intensity skill training, game IQ development, leadership sessions, and showcase games—built to move you up a level.

FAQs

High-intensity skill training, game IQ, leadership—and competitive reps that translate to real games.


Is the camp co-ed?

Yes. Both male and female student-athletes are welcome. Sessions group by age and skill level for competitive balance.

Do you offer scholarship opportunities?

Yes. Limited merit- and need-based scholarships may be available. You can indicate interest during registration.

Can beginners attend?

Absolutely. Coaches scale intensity and drills to each athlete’s level—everyone trains to improve.
