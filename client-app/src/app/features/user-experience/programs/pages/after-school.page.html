Parent FAQs

What ages and forms do you serve?

We work with students in Forms 3–6 (typically ages 11–18), providing the right mix of academic structure and athletic challenge for each stage of development.

Do you help with exam prep?

Yes. We support CSEC, CAPE, SAT, and school-based exam prep with curriculum-aligned lessons focused on understanding over memorization.

Is training sport-specific?

Absolutely. We design sport-specific training plans that balance skill work, strength, and coordination with academic responsibilities.

How do you track progress?

Each student-athlete has an individualized plan with measurable goals. We provide regular assessments, feedback sessions, and parent reports.
